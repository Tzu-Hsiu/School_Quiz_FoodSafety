# Food Safety Special Task Force

An interactive web game designed for 8th-grade students to learn food safety inspection skills through immersive, scenario-based challenges.

## Description

Players take on the role of a trainee food safety inspector at the **Food Safety HQ**. Across five progressively challenging levels, students learn to identify hygiene violations, read food labels, manage temperature risks, and make real-world dining decisions — all wrapped in a cyberpunk-themed UI with speed bonuses, XP tracking, and RPG-style mission briefings.

## Learning Objectives

- **Food hygiene inspection** — identify violations in kitchens, storage areas, and dining zones
- **Food labeling compliance** — determine whether product labels meet Taiwan's food safety regulations
- **Temperature danger zone** — understand the 7-60 °C danger zone and its real-world implications
- **Critical thinking under pressure** — weigh conflicting evidence and social pressure to make safe choices
- **Food additive literacy** — distinguish legal additives from genuine risks

## How to Play

1. Open `game/index.html` in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
2. Enter your name and class, then click **REGISTER**
3. Complete levels 1-5 in order — each level unlocks the next
4. Answer quickly for **Speed Bonus** points
5. Earn all 5 badges and score 340+ to become a **Certified Food Safety Inspector**

No server, build step, or installation required — it's a single HTML file.

## Game Levels

| Level | Title | Format |
|-------|-------|--------|
| 1 | Knowledge Assessment | 6 scenario-based multiple choice questions |
| 2 | On-Site Inspection | Investigate 10 zones in a virtual restaurant |
| 3 | Label Verification | Timed pass/fail judgment on 6 food products |
| 4 | Decision Simulation | Choose vendors for school events using investigation points |
| 5 | Certification Exam | 9-question timed final exam |

## Technologies Used

- **HTML5** — single-file application, no frameworks
- **CSS3** — cyberpunk theme with neon glow effects, animations, responsive design
- **Vanilla JavaScript** — game state management, DOM rendering, localStorage leaderboard
- **Google Fonts** — Orbitron (UI headings)

## Deployment

Host the `game/` folder on any static hosting service:

- **GitHub Pages** — push and enable in repo settings
- **Netlify** — drag-and-drop the `game/` folder
- **Any web server** — just serve `index.html`
